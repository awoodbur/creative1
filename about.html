About

Woodbury Wilderness has been operating since 2002. What started as a small adventure expedition start up has grown to be a nationally renowned adventure expedition hub! We proudly host expeditions in 24 states and have 1,200 amazing staff making it all possible! Our expeditions range from animal viewing, hiking, white water rafting, ziplining, and more!
