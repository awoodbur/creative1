FAQ

Q: How much does it cost to book a trip?

A: It depends on the location! Our cheapest trip to a location in your state could cost as little as $100!

Q: Where can we go on our trip?

A: We offer trip packages in several locations in every state. Usually our trips will visit the most prominent national parks closest to you!

Q: How many people can I bring?

A: We have a minimum party size of 2 and a maximum party size of 12!

Q: Are the trips dangerous?

A: Our trips are guided by professional nature explorers who will be with you every step of the way. We have never had a serious accident on any of our trips!

Q: Are the trips difficult?

A: We will modify our trips as necessary depending on the needs of the group to make sure it is enjoyable for everyone!
